## Mafia — Game Rules

### Overview

Mafia is a social deduction game between two factions: **Citizens** and **Mafia**. The game alternates between Day and Night phases. During the Day, all players discuss and vote to eliminate a suspect. During the Night, the Mafia secretly agree on a Citizen to eliminate.

---

### Players & Roles

Total **7 players** — 5 Citizens and 2 Mafia.

**Citizen** — Does not know any other player's role. Goal: identify and eliminate all Mafia members through the Day vote.

**Mafia** — Knows only their own role at game start. Learns the identities of fellow Mafia members during Night 1. Goal: eliminate enough Citizens to equal or outnumber them.

---

### Speaking Order

At game start, the Game Master assigns each player a fixed speaking position (1 through 7). This order is used in every Day Discussion phase. When a player is eliminated, their position is permanently skipped.

---

### Phase Sequence

#### Day 1 — Introductions

Each player, in speaking order, publicly introduces their character: a made-up **name**, **age**, and **occupation**. There is no discussion, accusation, or vote in this phase.

#### Night 1 — Mafia First Meeting

Mafia members meet in their private channel and introduce themselves to each other. No kill takes place this night.

#### Day N (N ≥ 2) — Discussion & Vote

**Discussion:** Each living player speaks once in fixed speaking order.

**Vote:** All living players simultaneously cast a public vote naming one player for elimination. A player may not vote for themselves and may not abstain. The player with the most votes is eliminated. On a tie, the tied player who spoke earliest in that day's Discussion phase is eliminated.

#### Night N (N ≥ 2) — Mafia Action

Mafia members meet in their private channel and discuss their target. They must reach a unanimous consensus before a kill is submitted. The private channel remains open until consensus is reached. The agreed target is then eliminated.

#### The cycle repeats: Day Discussion & Vote → Night Mafia Action → …

---

### Elimination

When a player is eliminated their role **is revealed** to all surviving players.

Eliminated players may observe all remaining phases but cannot speak, vote, or act in any channel.
